Hi my name is Sariah and I have some advice for you!

Resources are your friends!

Read once, do once, watch someone else do, do again, read again. That's how to learn.

Dig up good resources! Make a developer friend, favorite the ws3 site, and search, search, search!

Motivation plays an important role.

Mistakes happen. In fact, it's guaranteed.

It's ok to be frustrated. But you can fix it. Even when you delete all your files and didn't have backups, re-writing your code is MUCH faster the second time around! And you learn to back-up everything frequently!

Just remember why you wanted to do this in the first place...Better job? More security? The ability to make your own site? Whatever it is, it's worth it!
